CGPA stands for Cumulative Grade Point Average. It is a calculation of the average of all your grade points earned throughout your entire high school career. The CGPA is used by colleges and universities to determine your eligibility for admission and scholarships.
The higher your CGPA, the more likely you are to be admitted to the college or university of your choice and to receive scholarships.
